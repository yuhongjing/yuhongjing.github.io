## 前瞻
The essence of all time in the past， in the book.  
过去一切时代的精华尽在书中。  
Books are a great talent for the heritage of mankind.  
书籍是伟大的天才留给人类的遗产。  
Reading is to build up your mind with the help of other people's idea.  
读书是在别人思想的帮助下，建立自己的思想.
